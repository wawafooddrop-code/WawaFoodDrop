Privacy Policy

We take your data seriously. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Wawa Food Drop ("we," "our," or "Wawa") operates a food delivery platform connecting customers with restaurants and delivery riders in the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app and related services.

Important: By using Wawa Food Drop, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. What Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Give Us

  • Account details — Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Delivery address — Your default and alternate delivery addresses
  • Profile information — Profile photo, preferred cuisines, saved restaurants
  • Payment information — Cash on delivery (COD) details are handled directly; no payment card data is stored on our servers
  • Communications — Messages you send us, support tickets, feedback

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device data — Device type, operating system, browser, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — Pages visited, features used, order history, session duration
  • Location data — Approximate location (city-level) for restaurant recommendations; precise location only if you enable delivery tracking
  • Log data — IP address, access timestamps, referring URLs

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Restaurant partners — Menu items, pricing, and order details when you place an order
  • Delivery riders — Delivery confirmation, rider name/contact for active orders
  • Firebase/Google services — Anonymous analytics (non-personally identifying)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfill your food delivery orders from restaurant to doorstep
  • Communicate order status, estimated delivery times, and updates
  • Match you with available delivery riders and share relevant delivery details with them
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries or disputes
  • Send service-related notifications (order confirmations, rider en route alerts, promotional offers you opted into)
  • Improve our platform — app features, menu recommendations, delivery routing
  • Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or platform abuse
  • Comply with our legal obligations under Philippines law

3. Who We Share Your Information With

3.1 With Restaurants

When you place an order, we share your name and delivery address with the restaurant so they can prepare and fulfill your order. The restaurant's privacy practices govern their use of your data.

3.2 With Delivery Riders

When a rider accepts your order, we share your delivery address and contact number (masked) so they can complete the delivery. The rider is contractually obligated to use this information only for your delivery.

3.3 With Service Providers

We use Firebase (Google) for data storage, authentication, and analytics. Their Privacy Policy applies. We also use third-party SMS/notification services for order alerts.

3.4 Legal & Safety Disclosures

We will disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or where necessary to protect the safety, rights, or property of Wawa, our users, or the public.

3.5 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security & Storage

We use Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication — both provide encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only, governed by Firebase's enterprise-grade access controls.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to keep their account credentials confidential and to sign out on shared devices.

5. Your Rights

As a Wawa Food Drop user, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct — Update or fix inaccurate account information at any time via your Profile page
  • Delete — Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent — Opt out of promotional notifications at any time via Settings or the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Data portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@wawafooddrop.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies — Required for authentication and session management. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Anonymous usage data collected via Firebase Analytics. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • No advertising cookies — We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

7. Children's Privacy

Wawa Food Drop is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a minor's data has been collected, we will delete it immediately. Contact us at privacy@wawafooddrop.com if you believe a child has provided us with personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify you via a prominent notice on the app or by sending an email to your registered address.

Your continued use of Wawa Food Drop after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: privacy@wawafooddrop.com
  • In-app: Profile → Help & Support
  • Response time: Within 30 days of receiving a valid request.
